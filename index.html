Welcome to Preview It.

Sometimes you may find a link item in a page and you want to click it. But, yes, but, you may only want to have a glance at the page, and you don't want to open it at a new tab or windows, or sometimes horribly replace the current page (Oh shit!).

So here comes the Preview It (名字待定).

You can test here

Hover on the link you want to preview

Don't know what is hover? hover is putting your cursor onto the link, that means the cursor in within the link range.

Press the "w" button on your keyboard.

Don't have a keyboard? Oh, we will find some more fancy way to activate the function, after all it's in beta phase. Using the phone? Sorry, you are not able to use any kinds of extension.

interact with the preview box

Yes, you can simply scroll, click the links or even fill the forms in the preview box. You just need to regard it as a small window, just looks cute and not takes too much spaces.

When you don't want it anymore, just press "w" key again.

Just like nothing happened, no changes to your current page.
