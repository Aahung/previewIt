Welcome to S-Preview

Sometimes you may find a link item in a page and you want to click it. But, yes, but, you may only want to have a glance at the page, and you don't want to open it at a new tab or windows.

So here comes the S-Preview !!!

You can get it HERE!!!
You can test here

1. Hover on the link you want to preview

Don't know what is hover? hover is putting your cursor onto the link, that means the cursor in within the link range.

2. Press the "w" button on your keyboard.

Using the phone? Sorry, you are not able to use any kinds of extension.

3. interact with the preview box

Yes, you can simply scroll, click the links or even fill the forms in the preview box. You just need to regard it as a small window, just looks cute and not takes too much spaces.

It has 4 buttons on the left. The first 1 on the LEFT-TOP corner is for closing the window

The 2nd one is used to move the position of the small window. Just drag it by clicking on it!

The 3rd one is for sharing! You can share your favourate page through Facebook by clicking it!

The last one is for setting

4. When you don't want it anymore, just press "w" key again or check the close button.

The page on the top will disappear~
